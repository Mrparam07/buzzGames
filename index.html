Snake Game

The player controls a long, thin creature, resembling a snake, which roams around on a bordered plane, picking up food (or some other item), trying to avoid hitting its own tail or the edges of the playing area. Each time the snake eats a piece of food, its tail grows longer, making the game increasingly difficult.

Tic-Tac-Toe

Each player picks one word in turn and to win, a player must select three words with the same letter. The words may be plotted on a tic-tac-toe grid in such a way that a three-in-a-row line wins.

Highway Racing

A car game, car simulator, driving game, or racing game is a video game where the player controls a vehicle in a real-world or fantasy environment or track.

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is an arcade-style game in which the player controls the bird Faby, which moves persistently to the right. The player is tasked with navigating Faby through pairs of pipes that have equally sized gaps placed at random heights.

Candy Crush

In the game, players complete levels by swapping colored pieces of candy on a game board to make a match of three or more of the same color, eliminating those candies from the board and replacing them with new ones, which could potentially create further matches.

Running Dinosaur

The Dinosaur Game (also known as the Chrome Dino) is a browser game developed by Google and built into the Google Chrome web browser. The player guides a pixelated Tyrannosaurus rex across a side-scrolling landscape, avoiding obstacles to achieve a higher score.

Wach-A-Mole

The goal of whac-a-mole is to beat 36 total levels and get the highest score possible. For every 9 levels that you beat, the speed increases. There are a total of 4 different speeds. Only 6 mistakes are allowed before the game is over.

Doraemon Puzzle

Doraemon Puzzle Game is a fun and challenging puzzle game featuring characters from the popular Japanese manga and anime series. Players must use their problem-solving skills to complete puzzles of varying difficulty levels, with the help of power-ups and bonuses.

Black Jack

Blackjack is a popular card game that is played in casinos worldwide. The objective of the game is to have a hand value of 21 or as close to 21 as possible without going over. In the game, players compete against the dealer, not against each other

Get Home

Maze game is a type of puzzle game where the player has to navigate through a complex maze, with the objective of reaching the end point or finding a hidden object. The maze is usually depicted as a labyrinth of interconnected paths and obstacles, and the player has to use logic and strategy to find the right path.

Water Puzzle

The Water Puzzle game is a type of puzzle game where the objective is to move water from one location to another using various tools and obstacles. The player must use their problem-solving skills to navigate through each level and find the most efficient way to transport the water to its destination.

Rock Paper Scissors

The Stone Paper Scissors game, also known as Rock Paper Scissors, is a simple game typically played by two people. The game involves each player making a hand gesture that represents one of three objects: a rock (represented by a closed fist), a piece of paper (represented by an open hand), or a pair of scissors (represented by two fingers in a V-shape).
